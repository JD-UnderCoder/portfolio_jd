VIDEO BACKGROUND SETUP
======================

Place your looping background video here as: background.mp4

RECOMMENDED SPECS:
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- File Size: Under 5MB
- Style: Technology, particles, digital, space, abstract

FREE VIDEO SOURCES:
1. Pexels Videos - https://www.pexels.com/videos/
2. Pixabay - https://pixabay.com/videos/
3. Coverr - https://coverr.co/

SEARCH TERMS:
- "technology background"
- "digital particles"
- "circuit board"
- "abstract futuristic"
- "space stars"
- "matrix digital"

NOTE: If no video is present, the site will automatically show a gradient background fallback.

Your video should be named: background.mp4
